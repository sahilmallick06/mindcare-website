Wellness Resources

🧘‍♀️ Mindfulness & Breathing Exercises

Practice these simple yet effective exercises to reduce stress and anxiety:

  • 4-7-8 Breathing: Inhale for 4, hold for 7, exhale for 8 seconds
  • Progressive Muscle Relaxation: Tense and release each muscle group
  • 5-4-3-2-1 Grounding: Name 5 things you see, 4 you feel, 3 you hear, 2 you smell, 1 you taste
  • Body Scan Meditation: Focus attention on different parts of your body
